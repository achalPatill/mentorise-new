# 🚀 Mentorise

**Mentorise** is an AI-powered mentor-mentee platform designed to connect students, professionals, and industry experts through meaningful mentorship. The platform enables users to discover mentors, schedule sessions, communicate in real-time, participate in video meetings, and receive personalized AI-assisted guidance for learning and career growth.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👤 Authentication & Authorization

* Secure user registration and login
* JWT-based authentication
* Role-based access for Mentors and Mentees

### 🎯 Mentor Discovery

* Browse and explore mentor profiles
* View mentor expertise, skills, and experience
* Connect with mentors based on interests and goals

### 📅 Session Booking

* Book one-on-one mentorship sessions
* Accept or reject session requests
* Manage upcoming and past bookings

### 💬 Real-Time Chat

* Instant mentor-mentee communication
* Powered by Socket.IO
* Unread message tracking

### 🎥 Video Meetings

* Built-in video session support
* Seamless mentor-mentee interaction

### 🤖 AI-Powered Assistance

* Google Gemini AI integration
* Intelligent guidance and learning support
* Interactive AI chat experience

### 📧 Email Notifications

* Session confirmation emails
* Session rejection notifications
* Automated communication using Nodemailer

### ⭐ Reviews & Ratings

* Mentor feedback system
* Rating-based credibility
